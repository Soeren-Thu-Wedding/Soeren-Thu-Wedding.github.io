The Proposal

After being together for half a year, Thu started hinting that she was ready for marriage. Søren, having a Western mentality towards relationships, thought it was a bit too soon. After one more year (and many more hints), Søren decided he was ready to go ahead with the proposal. One problem though; he had terrible taste in jewelry. Wanting to make sure Thu got a ring she would be thrilled to wear for the rest of her life, he asked her to pick out the ring herself. Arguably a rational approach, but also incredibly unromantic. Fast forward 1.5 months. It is now 27 November 2021 and we have flown to Denmark for vacation. A formal wedding ceremony in Aarhus has been planned for 10 December 2021. And Søren has not proposed yet.

Without a better plan, he decided he would carry around the ring until the right moment presented itself. At a nice spot in Tivoli, Søren popped the question and we agreed to spend the rest of our lives together. Last minute too, because 7 days later the formal wedding took place.
